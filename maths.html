Maths & academic projects

My maths interests extend in two main directions: abstract algebra and maths education. I am of the firm conviction that the scientific community is really bad at communicating, and this is part of why mathematics has such a bad name. I will probably write a reflective piece on this, but for the time being, I recommend you read Paul Lockhart's A Mathematician's Lament.
