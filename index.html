About me

Hello, I am Sonia (please call me Sonia, not Sarah!) Firstly, I am currently a Mathematics student at the University of Manchester. My interests lie in Abstract Algebra (in particular, non-commutative algebra!) and Maths Education. Secondly, I have a lot of roles at the University: Library Student Team member, Student Rep Coordinator, Peer-assissted Study Sessions Coordinator, MathSoc committee member, and overall helper with departmental initiatives (this was a mouthful). Thirdly, I am passionate about maths, continental philosophy, reading, and writing. I hope that one day I will find a way to bridge these hobbies together. Until then, I will keep on exploring the world and myself!

Because I guessed that you would be wondering where my name is from - I am from Romania.

Maths Stuff
