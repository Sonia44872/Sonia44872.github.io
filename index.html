About Maths Non-maths

I’m Sonia, a Mathematics student at the University of Manchester interested in abstract algebra, mathematics education, and the intellectual history behind mathematical ideas.

Around the university, I work behind the scenes in roles such as PASS Coordinator, Student Rep Coordinator, Library Student Team member, MathSoc organiser, and part of the team running the Manchester Interdisciplinary Mathematics Undergraduate Conference.

Outside mathematics, I care deeply about writing, philosophy, and creative reflection. I’m originally from Romania, and Manchester has become the place where these interests slowly weave into a single thread.
